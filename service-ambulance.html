Advanced Life Support (ALS)

What To Do During a Medical Emergency

Stay calm and follow these steps while the ambulance is on its way

Call Immediately

Call 7848041123 or 108. Stay on the line and clearly state the patient's condition and your exact location.

Keep Patient Calm & Still

Do not move the patient unless there is immediate danger (e.g., fire). Keep them warm and reassured.

Clear the Area

Move bystanders away, unlock the main entrance/gate, and send someone to guide the ambulance to the exact location.

Do Not Give Food or Water

Unless instructed by the dispatcher, avoid giving the patient food, water, or medication before paramedics arrive.

Pre-book an Ambulance Slot

Planning a scheduled procedure or hospital visit? Pre-book our ambulance to guarantee availability on your chosen date and time.
