Why Free Checkups Matter

Many serious illnesses like diabetes, hypertension, and thyroid disorders show no early symptoms. Our free checkup program is designed to catch these conditions early — when they're most treatable — so you can take action before things get serious.

What's Included in Your Free Checkup

A full-body health screening covering the most critical parameters

Blood Pressure Check

Measures your systolic and diastolic pressure to detect hypertension or hypotension early. Takes less than 2 minutes.

Blood Sugar (Glucose) Test

Finger-prick glucose test to screen for pre-diabetes and diabetes. Fasting and random readings both available.

BMI & Body Composition

Height, weight, and Body Mass Index measurement with personalized ideal weight range and risk assessment.

Vision Screening

Basic eye test to detect near-sightedness, far-sightedness or early signs of eye problems requiring specialist care.

Respiratory Check

Stethoscope-based lung and chest examination to detect abnormal sounds, wheezing, or early signs of infection.

Temperature & General Vitals

Complete vitals recording: body temperature, pulse rate, SpO2 (oxygen saturation) and general wellness assessment.

Who Should Get Checked?

Free checkups are open to everyone — prioritized for high-risk groups

Adults 30+

All adults above 30 should get annual checkups even if feeling healthy, as many conditions are silent in early stages.

Children & Teens

Growth monitoring, vaccination tracking, and nutritional assessments for children of all age groups.

Pregnant Women

Special maternal health screenings including hemoglobin, blood pressure, and glucose monitoring during pregnancy.

Chronic Condition Patients

Patients with diabetes, hypertension, asthma or heart disease benefit most from regular monitoring checkups.
